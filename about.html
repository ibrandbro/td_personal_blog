About

This site is a personal blog focused on implementation-oriented research across financial crime, risk, payments, compliance architecture, and AI in regulated industries.

The goal is to translate industry and regulatory concepts into practical frameworks that organizations can use to think about systems, data requirements, ownership models, and execution.

Going forward, this site will be used to publish independent research, implementation blueprints, and observations across multiple industries.
