Where AI Fits in Regulated Workflows

AI is increasingly discussed in compliance and risk operations, but the better question is where it adds value without weakening control integrity.

Useful AI roles

  • Document extraction and normalization
  • Identity-data comparison and anomaly surfacing
  • Case summarization and narrative drafting
  • Research support for PEP and adverse media review

Where human review remains critical

  • High-impact sanctions decisions
  • EDD judgments
  • SAR filing approvals
  • Policy exceptions and governance
